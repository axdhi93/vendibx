Why We Started

VENDIBX was founded with one simple mission: to make high-protein, fresh, and affordable meals accessible to students and fitness enthusiasts.

As a college student, I noticed that many people wanted to eat healthy but struggled to find meals that were nutritious, reasonably priced, and convenient. Most options were either expensive, lacked protein, or weren't freshly prepared.

Instead of accepting the problem, I decided to build a solution.

Today, VENDIBX focuses on delivering freshly cooked, protein-rich meals made with quality ingredients — without unnecessary preservatives or complicated meal plans. Every meal is designed to help busy students, gym-goers, and working professionals stay on track with their fitness goals.

We're not just delivering food — we're building a community that believes healthy eating should be simple, affordable, and reliable.
